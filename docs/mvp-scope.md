# MVP scope

## In scope (initial release)

| Area | MVP intent |
|------|------------|
| Procurement | **RFQ**, **purchase orders**, receiving that feeds inventory |
| Inventory | Stock tracking via **movements**; alignment with procurement and sales |
| Sales | **Sales orders**, invoicing path, payment capture |
| Accounting | **AP** (supplier-side) and **AR** (customer-side) tied to operational documents |

## Out of scope (MVP)

- **Native mobile applications**
- **AI-driven analytics** or ML-heavy forecasting (can be revisited post-MVP)

## Notes

- Scope can be refined per sprint; treat this document as the **baseline** for what “first shippable ERP slice” means for JMC ERP.
